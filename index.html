History

Wiki Wars

Wiki Wars is a game where players challenge each other to navigate from one Wikipedia page to another using only hyperlinks. The goal is to reach the target page in the fewest clicks possible, testing knowledge, strategic thinking, and quick decision-making as participants explore the interconnected web of information on Wikipedia.
